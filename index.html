TIPS

vein test

WARM

if your veins appear greenish, you most likely have a warm undertone.

COOL

if your veins appear bluish or purple, you probably have a cool undertone.

NEUTRAL

if you can’t quite tell, or they look like a mix of both, you may have a neutral undertone.

jewelry test

WARM

if gold jewelry flatters you more, you likely have a warm undertone.

COOL

if silver jewelry looks better, you probably have a cool undertone.

NEUTRAL

if you look good in both, you may have a neutral undertone.
